Zombie Max File:

- Version 3dsMax 2010
- 1800 polys
- 512x512 Targa
- Biped Rig
- Detachable Limbs (Head, arms, legs)

====================================================================

To detach the limbs select the following:

- fk_Hand_L
- fk_Hand_R
- fk_Foot_L
- fk_Foot_R
- fk_Spine

And under the modifier panel turn the switch on and off.
